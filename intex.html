Historical Places in India


Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum located in Agra, built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Red Fort

The Red Fort is a historic fort in Delhi, famous for its massive red sandstone walls and beautiful architecture.

Hampi

Hampi is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its stunning ruins and temples that reflect the grandeur of the Vijayanagara Empire.

Qutub Minar

The Qutub Minar is a towering minaret in Delhi, famous for its intricate carvings and impressive height, standing at 73 meters.
